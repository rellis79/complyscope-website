Questions buyers ask first

Is this an AI system?
Yes. ComplyScope uses AI language models inside a controlled review workflow. The system checks documents against defined Irish regulatory standards and produces structured outputs for human review. It does not approve documents, make legal decisions, or replace the duty holder. For full details on how documents are handled, stored, and processed, see our Data Handling Statement.
Does ComplyScope approve or sign off documentation?
No. The system detects gaps and produces a report. Approval and sign-off remain entirely with the duty holder. Legal responsibility sits with a human, not with a system.
How is this different from an audit?
An audit issues a verdict. ComplyScope detects gaps and reports them. The duty holder reviews the findings and decides what the verdict is.
I already have a Safety Advisor. Why do I need this?
Your Safety Advisor needs to be on site — conducting inspections, managing real-time risks, talking to operatives. Every hour they spend at a desk reviewing incoming subcontractor paperwork is an hour they are not on site. ComplyScope handles the first-pass document review automatically. Your Safety Advisor reviews findings, not raw paperwork.
How does it handle AI errors or hallucinations?
Every output is framed as a gap report, not a verdict. Every report is reviewed by a duty holder before any action is taken. The system is built to produce consistent outputs on the same document every time.
How does the system connect to my document workflow?
Upload documents directly through the ComplyScope dashboard — Word or PDF files, any format. For automatic processing, ComplyScope connects to your existing SharePoint or OneDrive environment via Microsoft's standard API. Documents land in a designated folder, the system detects them automatically, and gap reports are returned to your output folder.
